About Us


Snapdeal is one of India's leading e-commerce platforms, focusing on providing value-for-money products to its customers.



Founded in 2010 by Kunal Bahl and Rohit Bansal, Snapdeal has become a prominent online lifestyle shopping destination in India. Over the past 14+ years, Snapdeal has served more than 100 million online shoppers, offering a wide range of fashion and lifestyle products that cater to diverse customer needs. Snapdeal's vision is to enable shoppers across India to experience the joy of living their aspirations through reliable,value-for-money shopping. By focusing on the aspirations of shoppers across the country, Snapdeal offers the latest trends in both fashion and non-fashion segments at competitive prices for value-savvy buyers. With a close understanding of consumer needs, a seamless shopping experience, and a pan-India footprint, Snapdeal continues to make online shopping convenient and accessible for millions of users every day.
